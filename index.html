About Rinker

More Than A Dorm

Rinker Hall is more than just a dorm, it is a brotherhood. At Rinker, you will build close relationships with not only your roommates, but your fellow brothers in Christ. Whether it is the annual Rinker Retreat, weekly Bible study, or just hanging out, you will always feel at home with the other guys living here.

The Facilities

Having just been renovated this past summer, Rinker Residence Hall has brand new dorm rooms and bathrooms. Each suite is setup with four two-person bedrooms, a shared bathroom with two showers, stalls, and sinks, as well as an amazing living room an balcony. There is also a sundeck for everyone to enjoy, which is accessible on the third floor. Are lobby is a great place to hangout, equipped with couches, a pool table, foosball, and flat-screen TV. There is also a mail/study-room, a prayer room, a laundry room (services provided with housing), and a kitchen, in the lobby.

What Are My Options?

Matthew 18

If you witness a friend, roommate, or other community member violating PBA policy, you have the ability to confront them, and hae that converstaion as peers. Always start there unless the violation goes against federal or state law, or if it causes danger or harm to someone. In those cases, please have a conversation with a member of the Residence Life Staff.

Amnesty

Struggling with something and want help? Request amnesty by talking with your RD about what that process looks like for things like alcohol use, substance use, sexual relationships, or any behaviour that conflicts with PBA community standards. You don't need to struggle alone. We want to walk with you along the way.

Email : amnesty@pba.edu
