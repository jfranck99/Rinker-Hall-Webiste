Rinker Hall Hours

If you live in Rinker Hall, you are able to have visitors between certain hours. For your first semester of residence, you will also have to be back at your rooms by the designated times for room checks.

Visitation

Sundays and Mondays : No Visitation

Tuesdays - Thursdays : 7 pm - 11:15 pm

Fridays and Staurdays : 7 pm - 1:15 am

Curfew

Sundays - Thursdays : 11:30 pm

Fridays and Saturdays : 1:30 am
